# NIP-19 Reference Card

### REFERENCE_CARD
NIP-19 standardizes bech32-formatted strings for displaying keys, ids, and other information. 
- **Bare keys and ids**: Use bech32 encoding with prefixes `npub` (public keys), `nsec` (private keys), and `note` (note ids).
- **Shareable identifiers with extra metadata**: Use bech32 prefixes `nprofile`, `nevent`, `naddr`, and `nrelay` with `TLV` (type-length-value) encoding.
- **TLV types**:
  - `0`: `special` (profile public key, event id, or identifier)
  - `1`: `relay` (optional, ascii-encoded relay)
  - `2`: `author` (pubkey of the event)
  - `3`: `kind` (32-bit unsigned integer, big-endian)
- **Examples**:
  - `npub` public key: `npub180cvv07tjdrrgpa0j7j7tmnyl2yr6yr7l8j4s3evf6u64th6gkwsyjh6w6`
  - `nprofile` with `TLV` items: `nprofile1qqsrhuxx8l9ex335q7he0f09aej04zpazpl0ne2cgukyawd24mayt8gpp4mhxue69uhhytnc9e3k7mgpz4mhxue69uhkg6nzv9ejuumpv34kytnrdaksjlyr9p`
- **Common use cases**: Displaying keys, ids, and other information in a human-friendly format, sharing profiles or events with relay information and metadata.
